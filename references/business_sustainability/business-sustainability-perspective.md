# 商业可持续性视角 — Business Sustainability Perspective

## Evaluation Criteria

### Business Model Alignment
- The tool's features match its business model
- Revenue model is clearly documented
- Customer segments are well-defined

### Cost Structure
- Infrastructure costs are reasonable
- Scaling costs are predictable
- Vendor lock-in is minimized

### User Retention
- Churn reasons are tracked
- Onboarding experience improves
- Power users are identified and nurtured

### Scalability Limits
- Known bottlenecks are documented
- Scaling path is clear
- Capacity planning is in place
