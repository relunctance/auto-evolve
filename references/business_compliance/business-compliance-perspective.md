# 商业合规视角 — Business Compliance Perspective

## Evaluation Criteria

### License Compliance
- All dependencies have compatible licenses
- License obligations are met
- No license conflicts

### Data Privacy (GDPR/CCPA)
- Consent mechanisms are implemented
- Data subject rights are supported
- Privacy policy is accurate

### Intellectual Property
- No unlicensed code or assets
- Open source contributions are properly attributed
- Trademarks are respected

### Contractual Obligations
- SLA terms are realistic
- Support obligations are clear
- Liability limits are defined
