# 市场影响视角 — Market Influence Perspective

## Evaluation Criteria

### Competitive Positioning
- Differentiation from similar tools is clear
- Unique value proposition is documented
- Competitor weaknesses are addressed

### User Sentiment
- User feedback is actively collected
- Pain points from reviews are tracked
- NPS/CSAT is monitored

### Release Rhythm
- Regular releases on a predictable schedule
- Changelog is maintained
- Version tagging is consistent

### Community Health
- Issue response time is reasonable
- Community contributions are valued
- Documentation improves over time
